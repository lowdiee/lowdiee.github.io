TEMPERATURE RECORDS

Historical extremes and interesting facts

LOWEST RECORDED
-89.2°C Vostok Station, Antarctica

• Recorded on July 21, 1983

• Coldest permanently inhabited place: Oymyakon, Russia (-67.7°C)

POLAND LOWEST
-41.0°C Siedlce, Poland

• Recorded on January 11, 1940

• Winter 1928/29 was coldest in Polish history

HIGHEST RECORDED
56.7°C Furnace Creek, USA

• Recorded on July 10, 1913

• Death Valley averages 47°C in July

POLAND HIGHEST
40.2°C Pruszków, Poland

• Recorded on July 19, 2023

• 2019 was hottest year in Polish history

DID YOU KNOW?

• The fastest temperature rise: 27°C to -2°C in 15 minutes (Spearfish, USA)

• Poland's climate is getting warmer - average temp rose by 1.7°C since 1950

• Coldest capital city: Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia (avg. -1.3°C)
